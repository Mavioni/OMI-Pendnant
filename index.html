FAQ

Frequently asked questions

The OMI Pendant is a small wearable AI device you wear around your neck. It contains a microphone, Bluetooth radio, and battery. When activated, it records audio from your environment and streams it to the OMI companion app on your phone, where AI models transcribe, summarize, and surface insights in real time.
Privacy is fundamental. The device only records when you press the button — there is no passive, always-on listening. All firmware is open source and auditable on GitHub. You choose which AI provider processes your audio. With the Starter plan, audio goes directly to your own API key; it never touches our servers.
The app supports iOS 16+ and Android 10+. On the AI side, you can connect your own OpenAI, Groq, or Deepgram API keys. The Pro plan uses our managed infrastructure (powered by best-in-class models) so you don't have to manage keys at all.
In active recording mode, expect 8–10 hours per charge. In standby (Bluetooth connected but not recording), the pendant lasts up to 48 hours. Charging from empty to full takes about 60 minutes via USB-C.
Absolutely. The OMI SDK is fully open and documented. You can build webhooks, custom triggers, integrations with tools like Notion or Zapier, or entirely new AI apps. The Developer plan includes full SDK access and a private Discord channel for builders.
